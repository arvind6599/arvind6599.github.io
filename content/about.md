I'm Arvind — an engineer and researcher who likes building things at the intersection of AI, math, and product.

Currently building AI workflows for law firms at [DeepJudge](https://www.deepjudge.ai/), where I work on making legal research faster and more reliable with LLMs.

## Background

I did my undergrad and grad work focused on machine learning and quantitative methods. My research touched on risk metrics in finance and quantum-classical hybrid algorithms — both of which required getting comfortable with a lot of math I didn't know going in.

Since then I've spent most of my time building applied AI systems: retrieval pipelines, LLM-powered tools, and the boring-but-important infrastructure that makes them actually work in production.

I also participate in hackathons fairly regularly. You can see projects on [Devpost](https://devpost.com/arvind-menon/).

## What I'm interested in

- Writing — I'm working on getting better at essays and longer-form thinking
